Instructions

On the left is the canvas where the simulation is rendered. On the right are two GUI panels. The first lists a couple controls for the program. You can add/remove a cell stroke, adjust the brush size, and start the simulation. The second lists the current cells that will be simulated as well as a couple properties that can be edited. The last cell stroke in the list is the current one that will be drawn on the canvas.

Click the simulation button to toggle the simluation. You can continue to edit the properties of the cells and even add more strokes in real time.

The isSolid property will treat the cell stroke as a static object that will not move during the simulation. Density affects whether the fluid should sink below other fluids or not.
